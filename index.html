Advantages of Bottled Water

-Bottled water provides a superior form of hydration.

-Bottled water is a product of convenience.

-Bottled water can store for a long time.

-Bottled water is guaranteed to be of a specific quality.

-Bottled water is a highly regulated product.

-Bottled water offers several different options from which to choose.

Disadvantages of Bottled Water

-Bottled water will always cost more than tap water.

-Bottled water can still create health problems.

-Bottled water might still have contaminants in it.

-Bottled water creates a recycling problem for most communities.

-Bottled water leaves a significant carbon footprint.

Facts About Bottled Water

-One Million Water Bottles Are Purchased Every Minute.

-Bottled water is almost 2,000 times more energy intensive to produce than tap water.

-As of 2017, the global bottled water market was worth $198.5 billion, set to reach $300 billion by 2024.

-Reusable Bottles Tend to Have a Larger Opening.

-Bottled Water Brands Grew from 50 to 195 since 1998.
